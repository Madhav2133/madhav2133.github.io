TCAS System


(ROS2, Gazebo, C++/Python, TurtleBot3, ROS-Nav2) Multi-robot monitoring system called TCAS, which monitors multiple robots in a given arena and identify potential junction regions where robots might converge and to regulate their passage to prevent any close proximity interactions.

Checkout more at: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/reye_junction-monitoring-system/

AUV Nemo


Worked in the developement of Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) at AUV Society, IIITDMK. Nemo is an Underwater Vehicle with 6 DOF which contains a 3D Visualised Oak-D Series 3 Camera which helps in its autonomous navigation for performing various tasks required for the competition. This bot was built for participating in the event Singaport AUV Challange (SAUVC) in the year 2022.

Checkout more at: https://auviiitdm.github.io/

ROV Marty


Worked in the developement of Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) at AUV Society, IIITDMK. ROV Marty is an hybrid vehicle which can work both as an AUV and ROV. Its designed in such a way that with simple modifications we can convert it from one model to another so based on the requirements of the competition, we can make sure the bot can be ready. This bot was built in the year 2023 for the MATE-ROV World Championship 2023 which is being held in Longmont, CO.

Checkout more at: https://auviiitdm.github.io/

Traff Analyser


Studies the traffic conditions through different sensor data at a location and gives out the traffic trends. Based on traffic data collected from a sensor placed at a junction for specific amount of time, it studies the data and tell us the traffic trends from the collected information. We have collected data from four junctions and have displayed the trends of those with help of our CNN model.
